Vaporball VR

About The Project

Vaporball VR is a frantic multiplayer VR dodgeball game, where two teams are pitted against each other to collect the most gachapon capsules. It is networked using Photon Pun 2, and created over the course of two months.

Tech Used:
  • Unity Game Engine
  • Open XR
  • Photon Pun 2
My Tasks:
  • Networking systems
  • Score gameplay system
  • Player movement
  • VR Keyboard
  • Scriptable Object event system
  • Networked Sound effect system

My role in this project was in creating the gameplay and networking systems, while my partners Erickson Paschke and Matthew Feliciano who worked on Design and Gameplay elements respectively.
